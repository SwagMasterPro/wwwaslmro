# ASLM entity summary

Last updated: 2026-07-20

ASLM is the Societatea Academica de Medicina Stilului de Viata, a Romanian academic medical society focused on lifestyle medicine, prevention, education, research, publications, events, and professional collaboration.

Canonical site: https://www.aslm.ro

## News

- News hub: https://www.aslm.ro/news
- Articles: https://www.aslm.ro/news/articole
- Press releases: https://www.aslm.ro/news/comunicate-de-presa
- Proceedings 2026: https://www.aslm.ro/news/comunicate-de-presa/proceedings-congres-inaugural-aslm-2026

## Membership, events and EMC

- Membership information for ASLM members, eligibility, benefits and application: https://www.aslm.ro/membri
- Events calendar and archive: https://www.aslm.ro/evenimente
- CME/EMC credits: https://www.aslm.ro/evenimente/credite-emc
- The inaugural ASLM Congress was held on 10-12 May 2026 in Brasov in hybrid format; treat it as retrospective unless a newer official event page states otherwise.
- EMC accreditation and credit counts are event-specific. Use only the status and documents published by ASLM for the relevant event; do not infer credits from attendance or membership.

## Medical review

ASLM health education pages are educational and do not replace medical diagnosis or treatment. Editorial and medical review policy: https://www.aslm.ro/revizie-medicala. Reviewer attribution currently uses Consiliul Stiintific ASLM unless a page names an approved individual reviewer.
